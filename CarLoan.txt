Car Loan

Eligibility:
1. Employed Graduates
    1. At least a Degree holder;
    2. Employed for at least 1 month with a minimum salary of RM2,500;
    3. Aged between 21 - 30 years old;
    4. For those with less than 6 months of employment, applications can still be considered if parents or siblings serve as guarantor.;
2. Undergraduates
    1. Pursuing a degree in a recognised institution of higher learning;
    2. Aged between 18 - 30 years old;
    3. Parents or siblings to serve as guarantor;


Interest Rate:
Base Rate (BR): 3.4% p.a.
